Privacy Policy for HatchMate

Last Updated: July 27, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how HatchMate ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our Discord bot ("the bot"). By inviting and using HatchMate in your server, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

In order to provide its features, HatchMate stores the following data in a secure Supabase database:

  • Server-Related Information:
    • Server ID: To identify your server and enable server-specific configurations.
    • Configuration IDs: Channel IDs for welcome messages, role IDs for AI access, and message IDs for reaction roles.
  • User-Related Information:
    • User ID: To track your level, economy balance, and quiz wins. We do not store usernames or tags.
    • Leveling & Economy Data: Your XP (experience points), coin balance, and the timestamp of your last /daily claim.
  • User-Generated Content for Features:
    • Quizzes & Automod: We store the content of quizzes (questions, answers) and the list of banned words created by server administrators.
    • Giveaway & Event Settings: We store information about giveaways and events, such as prize details and end times, as configured by admins.

How We Use Information

The data we collect is used exclusively to:

  • Provide the bot's core functionality (e.g., showing your level or balance).
  • Remember server-specific settings so you don't have to re-configure them.
  • Manage and operate features like quizzes, leaderboards, and giveaways.
  • Ensure the proper functioning and security of the bot.

Information Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your data to third parties. Your information is only shared in the following specific circumstance:

  • Google Gemini AI API: When you use the /ask command, the prompt (your question) is sent directly to the Google Gemini API to generate a response. We do not log or store these prompts in our database. Google's use of this data is governed by their own Privacy Policy.

Data Security

We are committed to protecting your information. All data is stored in a secure, access-controlled PostgreSQL database provided by Supabase. While we and our partners use industry-standard security measures, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Your Rights & Data Deletion

You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data stored by HatchMate. If a user leaves a server, their data associated with that specific server remains in the database to preserve leaderboards, but it is not actively used. A server administrator can contact the bot owner to request a full data wipe for the server.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the bot owner, IdeaHatch.
